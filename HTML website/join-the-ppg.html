Join the Unsworth Group PPG

Our Patient Participation Group (PPG) is made up of patients who work with the practice to help improve services and support our local community. We welcome your involvement, whether you'd like to volunteer or simply stay informed through our newsletter.

Get Involved

We run small volunteer activities throughout the year, such as helping with practice events and supporting with tasks like managing parking during busy clinics, including COVID vaccination sessions. Whether you have time to volunteer occasionally or would simply like to stay informed through our newsletter, your involvement is welcome and valued.
