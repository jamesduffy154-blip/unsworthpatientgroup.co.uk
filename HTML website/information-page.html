Welcome to Unsworth Group Patient Participation Website

The Unsworth Patient Participation Group is dedicated to advertising local GP support and changes while sharing local resources. Our unique selling point is providing updates on the health news in Westhoughton and Blackrod. This is a great space to provide more information about our group and the services we offer on a regular basis.
