Members section

Welcome to your Unsworth Group PPG members area

Welcome to the Unsworth Patient Participation Group (PPG) Members Area. This section brings together important resources, updates, and opportunities for our members in one convenient place. Here you can access the latest PPG news, newsletters, feedback opportunities, volunteer information, event details, and local health and wellbeing resources. Designed to complement the rest of the Unsworth PPG website, this area provides clear navigation, an easy-to-use layout, and a welcoming environment that supports patient involvement and community engagement.

Whether you want to stay informed, help at events, share your views, or explore the latest community news, this section is your starting point.
